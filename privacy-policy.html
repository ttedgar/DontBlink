Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Don't Blink ("we", "us", "our"), available at dontblink.click, collects and uses information when you play our game.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information:

  • Display name — if you choose to enter a name to save your score to the leaderboard.
  • Reaction time scores — your game results (in milliseconds) submitted to the leaderboard.
  • Usage data — anonymous analytics data such as pages visited, session duration, and device type, collected automatically via Google Analytics.

We do not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to play.

How We Use Your Information

  • To display your name and score on the public leaderboard.
  • To analyse how the game is used and improve the experience.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase (Google) — used for data storage (leaderboard scores) and website hosting. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google Analytics — used for anonymous usage analytics. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google AdSense — used to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. Google Ads Policy

Cookies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies through Google Analytics and Google AdSense. You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings.

Leaderboard Data

Names and scores submitted to the leaderboard are stored in Firebase Firestore and are publicly visible to all players. Do not submit your real name or any personal information as your display name.

Children's Privacy

Don't Blink is a general-audience game suitable for ages 8 and up. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has submitted personal information, please contact us and we will remove it.

Data Retention

Leaderboard entries are retained indefinitely unless you request removal. Analytics data is retained according to Google's standard retention policies.

Your Rights

You may request that your leaderboard entry (name and score) be deleted at any time by contacting us. We will action the request within 30 days.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
